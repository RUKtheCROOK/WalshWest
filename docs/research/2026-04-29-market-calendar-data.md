## Recommendation for Implementation

When transcribing into TypeScript arrays:

1. **Mark estimated dates clearly** — prefix with "est." or include a `confirmed: false` boolean field for pattern-based dates (Employment Situation June-October, PPI June-October, Short-Term Energy Outlook June-October).

2. **COT is recurring** — model as a rule ("every Friday 3:30 p.m. ET") rather than listing 26 individual dates. Flag that holiday weeks may shift the schedule.

3. **Crop Progress is recurring** — model as "every Monday 4:00 p.m. ET, April-November" or list the specific 2026 Mondays sourced above.

4. **Weekly Petroleum / Natural Gas** — model as recurring ("every Wednesday 10:30 a.m. ET" / "every Thursday 10:30 a.m. ET") with explicit overrides for the two holiday-shifted petroleum dates (May 28, Sep 10) and the two holiday-shifted gas dates in November (outside this window but noted for pattern consistency).

5. **Link each entry to its source URL** — users clicking through should land on the authority that published the date, not a third-party aggregator (where possible).

6. **Add disclaimer text** — "Release dates are subject to change due to government shutdowns, appropriation lapses, or agency rescheduling. Verify with the publishing agency before relying on any specific date."
